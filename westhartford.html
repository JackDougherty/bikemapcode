bikemapcode displays mobile-friendly cycling maps on your website with easy-to-modify open-source code. Distributed as-is with no warranty under an MIT license. Feedback and code contributions welcome on GitHub or email to jack.dougherty@trincoll.edu


Select map layers:

  • Basemaps include Open Cycle Map, a user-edited layer to show marked bike lanes and facilities from Open Street Map; and ESRI Streets.
  • Overlay maps can show bike rides from GPX or KML files, or route networks from ArcGIS server.
